What Is EMI?

EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) is the fixed payment made every month by a borrower to repay a loan. It includes both principal and interest components over the loan tenure.

How Is EMI Calculated?

EMI is calculated using loan amount, interest rate, and tenure. A higher interest rate or longer tenure increases the total interest payable, while a shorter tenure increases monthly EMI.
