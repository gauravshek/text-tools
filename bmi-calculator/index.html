What is BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is calculated using your height and weight. It helps determine whether you are underweight, normal, overweight, or obese.

BMI Healthy Range

  • Below 18.5 – Underweight
  • 18.5 – 24.9 – Normal (Healthy)
  • 25 – 29.9 – Overweight
  • 30 and above – Obese
