What Is GST?

Goods and Services Tax (GST) is an indirect tax in India applied to the supply of goods and services. It replaces multiple indirect taxes and simplifies taxation across states.

Difference Between CGST, SGST and IGST

  • CGST: Central GST collected by the central government
  • SGST: State GST collected by the state government
  • IGST: Integrated GST applied to inter-state transactions
